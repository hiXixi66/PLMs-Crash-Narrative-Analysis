NASS-CDS (1979-2015) / CISS (2016-): in-depth data, used for crashworthiness analysis

NASS-CDS Ð National Automotive Sampling System - Crashworthiness Data System
A probability sample of tow-away crashes involving light vehicles
Data from 1979 to 2015 are available at: https://www.nhtsa.gov/content/nhtsa-ftp/179871

NASS-CDS is now replaced by CISS, see below
Includes in-depth information about approximately 3000-4500 crashes annually:
Crush-based estimation of Delta-V using WinSMASH
AIS coding of injuries

CISS: Crash Investigation Sampling System
Data from 2017 to 2023 are available at: https://www.nhtsa.gov/content/nhtsa-ftp/206

Case viewer for NASS-CDS and CISS allows filtering of crashes to find cases of interest

Manuals, fact sheets, statistics, etc. are available at:
https://crashstats.nhtsa.dot.gov
